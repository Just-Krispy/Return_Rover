From the Maker

Why I built this

K
Krispy
Maker

I was drowning in notes. Hundreds of bookmarks I'd never revisit. Podcast episodes I'd forget by lunch. Research papers that disappeared into folders.


The problem wasn't capturing information — it was connecting it. That article about AI agents and that podcast about farm lending? They had a connection I'd never have found manually.


So I built Second Brain: an intelligence system that captures, connects, and surfaces insights from everything I consume. It runs 24/7 on a VPS, has an AI bot (Archer) that manages research pipelines, and renders all my knowledge as an interactive 3D graph.


The transformation: I went from "I know I read something about this" to "here are the 5 most relevant notes, synthesized into an answer" — in seconds.

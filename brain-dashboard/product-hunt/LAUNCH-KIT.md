## Maker Comment (Post this on launch day)

**Who it's for:** Knowledge workers, researchers, and lifelong learners who consume more information than they can process.

**The problem:** We all save bookmarks, highlight articles, and take notes — then never connect them. The average knowledge worker spends 5+ hours/week re-finding information they've already seen.

**The transformation:** Second Brain captures everything automatically (RSS, podcasts, YouTube, Discord), connects notes using AI, and lets you search your own knowledge base with natural language. The 3D knowledge graph shows you patterns you'd never spot in a flat file list.

**What makes it different:**
- Runs 24/7 on your own server (no SaaS lock-in)
- 19 automation workflows that work while you sleep
- Overnight Scholar scrapes Reddit, GitHub, arXiv for your topics
- Built on Obsidian — your notes are just markdown files

Try the live demo: https://just-krispy.github.io/Return_Rover/brain-dashboard/
